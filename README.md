# StudyBuddyAl
StudyBuddy Al is a lovable, intelligent, and disciplined Al-powered study companion designed to help students study smarter, stay consistent, reduce stress, and perform better in exams.  It works as a planner, coach, friend, and accountability partner, providing personalized study plans, motivation, and progress tracking.
